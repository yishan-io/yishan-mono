This policy should be reviewed by your legal counsel before production use.

## 1. Purpose

This agreement allows a company to authorize contributions by its personnel to the YISHAN project while granting the project maintainer rights needed for source-available distribution and commercial licensing.

## 2. Parties

- Contributing company: the company on whose behalf a contributor submits changes
- Maintainer legal entity: the repository owner/maintainer of YISHAN
- Effective date: the date the relevant contribution is submitted

The contributing company is responsible for ensuring contributors acting on its behalf are authorized to submit contributions and accept this CCLA.

- Elastic License 2.0 (current project license), and

## 5. Ownership

Contributing company retains ownership of its Contributions, subject to the rights granted in this agreement.

## 6. Company Representations

Contributing company represents that:

- It has legal authority to enter this agreement.
- Authorized contributors are permitted to submit Contributions.
- To its knowledge, Contributions do not intentionally include material that infringes third-party IP rights.

Contributions are provided "AS IS", without warranties or conditions of any kind.
